Our Story

How We Met

We have known each other since Clay moved to Brookland in the fourth grade. We became close friends in junior high and into high school. We started dating at the beginning of senior year of high school and got engaged sophomore year of college! Our families have been good friends for a long time so it was meant to be all along :)

Proposal

We booked a cruise for the Spring Break of 2016 with Clay's family. On Monday night of the cruise it was formal night so we attended the dinner and after we had some time before the show we were wanting to go to was supposed to start. Clay lead me to the back of the boat where the view was beautiful and there was no one around. He took me to one of the chairs and began telling me how much I meant to him and eventually led into how he wanted to spend the rest of his life with me. He then got down on one knee and asked the question! I was so surprised which made it even better!
